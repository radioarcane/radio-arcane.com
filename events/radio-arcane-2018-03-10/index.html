Radio Arcane : Mittelmarch : The Dark Market

Saturday March 10, 2018 @ 7:00 PM – 3:00 AM

Mittelmarch: A Celebration of Faerie, plus National Mario Day!

Live dj's, Large Dance Area, Full Bar

Specialty Mario World Themed Drinks!

SNES and Wii available to play Mario Games on large screens in celebration!

Come dressed as your favorite Mario World Character or your favorite Faerie Costume. Of course, all are welcome regardless of appearance.

The Dark Market: A Curated Nightime Oddities and Art/Flea Market.

This event is expanding! More art, more activities and hopefully a FOOD TRUCK!
Free Entry from 7pm until 10pm. $5 entry after 10pm. 18+ to enter Full bar for 21+
