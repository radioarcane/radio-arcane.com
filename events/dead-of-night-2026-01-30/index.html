Friday January 30, 2026 @ 9:00 PM – 2:00 AM

Radio Arcane Presents Dead Of Night & The Dark Market: Dungeon Crawl - featuring Bad Milk Dirty Peache

The Nightmare continues on Friday, January 30th as Dead Of Night & The Dark Market keep up the monthly grind of dark eclectic music. Come out and help keep the dancefloor barely alive as we celebrate the glum drudgery of our dreadful existence.

This month will feature:

  • Bad Milk Dirty Peaches - An independent electronic project centered on ambient fantasy synth, built from layered synthesizers, lo-fi production, and restrained rhythmic elements. The music emphasizes dark atmosphere and texture, often using minimal structures and subtle variation. https://badmilkdirtypeaches.bandcamp.com
  • Radio Arcane Dark Music Specialists - Count Grozny, Motuvius Rex
  • Vending by the talented Dark Market.
  • Food - The Gingerbread Hag
  • Drinks by the enchanting Lany StarDust & Smooth T
