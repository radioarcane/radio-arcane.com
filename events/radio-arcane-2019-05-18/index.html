Radio Arcane : Goth Prom : The Dark Market

Saturday May 18, 2019 @ 7:00 PM – 3:00 AM

16th annual Goth Prom.

Radio Arcane (Louisville's Dark Music Specialists) partner once again with the premier night time oddities and art market, The Dark Market!

Shop the market for your favorite night time oddities and support local artists, then stay for dancing and merriment!

DJs, a professional Photo Booth, King and Queen competition*, various vendors of art and curiosities, cash bar, and much more fun and surprises!

Live performance by Love Under Will at 9pm.
https://loveunderwill.bandcamp.com

Doors open at 7pm Market @ 7pm, Party @ 10pm

18+
21+ to drink
$5 cover, free before 10pm

*Anyone can nominate a candidate for one King and/or one Queen and can vote for one King and/or one Queen. The nominees must agree to their nomination, and can choose if they are running for King or Queen. There is no gender requirement for either King or Queen. Nomination and voting will take place the night of the event. Thems the rules.
